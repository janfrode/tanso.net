Denali / Mount McKinley / Bolshaya Gora (Большая Гора) 2006 -- West Buttress

These are the waypoints collected while climbing Denali's West Buttress in June 2006. The team consisted of me (Jan-Frode Myklebust, age 31), Olav Glosli age 36, Helge Larsen age 41 and expedition leader Petter Bjørstad. We all summitted on June 14., after 14 days on the mountain! A detailed report (with pictures) can be read at Petter Bjørstad's impressive webpages.
